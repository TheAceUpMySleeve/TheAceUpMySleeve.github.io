The Nurburgring

The Nurburging is a race track in Nuremburg Germany, famous for being the longest race track in the world. Nicknammed "The Green Hell", This track is known for its famous twists and turns, and notorious for being a challenge for drivers for all skills. Formula 1 used to drive on this track, but with the massive elevation changes, it was too dangerous and they stopped racing on this track. This track is used today with the GT championship, and LMDH class.

BMW Museum

The BMW Museum is an automotive museum located in Munich Germany. It holds over 75 years of history from the car manufacturere BMW. Its a nuique experience to be able to walk around and watch automotive technology and design evolve with age.
